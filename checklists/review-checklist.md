# Review Checklist

## Content
- [ ] Follows the fixed structure (Executive Summary through References) with no sections skipped
- [ ] Every claim/statistic has a numbered citation
- [ ] "Limitations" section is substantive, not a token paragraph
- [ ] No sales language, no superlatives, no KryoNex product mentions in the body
- [ ] Technical accuracy reviewed by someone with relevant domain experience
- [ ] All diagrams referenced in text exist and are numbered correctly

## Structural
- [ ] Front-matter metadata complete (id, title, author, dates, tags)
- [ ] Author line and footer match editorial/brand-style-guide.md exactly
- [ ] File placed correctly under publications/{id}/
- [ ] Figures follow {id}-F{NN} numbering and live in publications/{id}/figures/
- [ ] CHANGELOG.md exists with a Version 1.0 entry at first publication
- [ ] review-notes.md items are all resolved or explicitly deferred before publish
- [ ] Content in the draft traces back to research-log/ entries, not invented during writing

## Sourcing
- [ ] Every source meets the tier bar in editorial/editorial-guidelines.md
- [ ] All links checked and working
- [ ] Citation formatting matches templates/citation-style.md
